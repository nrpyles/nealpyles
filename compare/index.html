Comparison FAQ

Common questions.

Is FHA or conventional better for me?

It depends on your credit, down payment, and how long you'll keep the home. FHA often wins on rate and upfront cash, conventional often wins long term because mortgage insurance drops off. That's exactly the tradeoff this tool shows you in dollars, on your numbers instead of someone's blog post.

How accurate are the closing costs?

The fee schedule mirrors real Lone Star Financing fee worksheets and Texas promulgated title insurance rates, so the totals land very close to an actual quote. Your official numbers come on a Loan Estimate after you apply.

What does this cost and what's the catch?

Nothing and there isn't one. No signup, no email required, nothing saved anywhere except your own browser. If the numbers raise questions, I'm one call away.
